Attracting Turtles

Have a pond with slope entrance.

Create Leaf litter piles.

Create basking features.

Plant Leafy plants and berries.

Repelling Turtles

Fence in plants that turtles prey on.

Use pond netting

Use a turtle cage to relocate turtles.

Be sure you find any possible entrances to your yard and close them off.

Call professional help for snapping turtles and relocating turtles.
